Offer · AI Readiness & ROI Audit

A clear roadmap for AI adoption—tied to outcomes your leadership can defend

This audit is for organizations that want a practical assessment of where AI can help, what should wait, and how to sequence work for measurable impact. You leave with priorities, a scorecard lens, and a 90-day plan—not a generic tool list.

Book Strategy Call

Best when you have cross-functional access to workflows and systems owners—or a willingness to coordinate short discovery sessions.

Who it’s for

If your organization matches one of these profiles, the audit is designed to meet you where you are.

Nonprofits

Teams balancing stewardship, compliance, and stretched capacity—where reporting and coordination quietly consume mission time.

Service businesses

Firms where delivery quality depends on handoffs—quotes, scheduling, client communications, and follow-up consistency.

Growing teams

Organizations hitting operational bottlenecks as volume grows—where tool sprawl and unclear ownership slow everyone down.

What’s included

Deliverables you can hand to leadership—written for decisions, not jargon.

  • Workflow and process review

    Where work actually happens, where data breaks, and where automation helps—or would add risk.

  • AI opportunity mapping

    Prioritized use cases aligned to outcomes: revenue, time, cost, quality, and reporting clarity.

  • Tool stack recommendation

    Fit-for-purpose guidance based on constraints: security, budget, adoption, and maintenance capacity.

  • Prioritized automation opportunities

    A sequenced list with rationale: what to pilot first, what to defer, and what to solve without AI.

  • KPI / ROI scorecard

    Baseline signals and simple tracking so progress is visible—not argued from anecdotes.

  • 90-day roadmap

    Concrete next steps, owners, and checkpoints—so the plan survives the first busy month.

What outcomes clients should expect

No inflated promises—just clarity and direction you can execute.

Identify revenue opportunities

Tighten follow-up, improve proposal speed, and reduce leakage from inconsistent processes.

Uncover time savings

Expose repetitive work that quietly consumes hours each week across roles.

Reduce operational friction

Make handoffs smoother between people, tools, and teams—fewer “where is this?” moments.

Improve visibility for leadership

Align metrics and reporting to decisions executives need to make—weekly and monthly.

How it works

A short timeline with crisp milestones—so teams know what to expect.

  1. Discovery

    Stakeholder interviews, workflow walkthroughs, and a practical inventory of constraints and priorities.

  2. Assessment

    Evaluation of opportunities, risks, data readiness, and adoption realities—grounded in how your team works today.

  3. Recommendations

    Prioritized plan with tool-fit guidance, automation candidates, KPI definitions, and a 90-day roadmap.

  4. Roadmap walkthrough

    A leadership session to align on sequencing, ownership, and what “success” means for the next quarter.
