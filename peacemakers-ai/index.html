FAQ

Straight answers for teams evaluating AI help seriously.

What is a fractional CEAI?

A fractional Chief Executive AI (CEAI) leader provides part-time strategic direction for how AI is adopted across the organization: priorities, governance, workflow design, vendor fit, and how results are measured—without requiring a full-time executive hire.

How is this different from AI consulting?

Many consulting engagements stop at recommendations. Peacemakers AI is built around outcomes: prioritized workflows, implementation guidance, enablement, and reporting. The goal is operational change you can see—not a binder on a shelf.

Do you implement tools or just advise?

Both, depending on what you need. We advise on what to do and why, and we support practical implementation in ways that match your team: configuration, workflow design, documentation, training, and measurement. If hands-on access is limited, we still make execution concrete and accountable.

What kinds of businesses are a good fit?

Organizations with real operational load—nonprofits, service businesses, and growing teams—where time, revenue, cost, and reporting clarity matter. The best fit is leadership that wants measurable change and is willing to align people and process—not chase novelty.

How do you measure ROI?

We define baseline signals up front (time, throughput, error rates, revenue proxies, cost of manual work), then track changes with a simple scorecard leadership can understand. AI ROI is treated as business ROI—not model benchmarks.

Do you replace employees with AI?

No. The point is to remove repetitive work and improve quality of decisions—so people spend time on judgment, relationships, and mission-critical tasks. Roles evolve; they are not treated as disposable shortcuts.
